Jianguo HAO



Postdoctoral Researcher at the National Institute of Health Data Science at Peking University.

Jianguo Hao is currently a postdoctoral researcher at the National Institute of Health Data Science at Peking University, working with Prof. Luxia Zhang. He obtained his Ph.D. in Computer Science and Mathematics from the University of Québec in 2019 and his M.A.S. degree in Artificial Intelligence and Decision from Sorbonne University in 2014. His research interests focus on multi-modal medical data analysis, biomedical knowledge discovery, and clinical decision support systems.
